Discover Korea

Explore the rich culture, historic landmarks, and natural beauty of Korea

Top Attractions in Korea

Gyeongbokgung Palace

The grandest of Seoul's Five Palaces, offering a glimpse into Korea's royal past with its beautiful architecture and changing of the guard ceremony.

  • Location: Seoul
  • Best Time: Spring for cherry blossoms
  • Activity: Palace tours, museum visits

N Seoul Tower

Iconic tower perched on Namsan Mountain offering panoramic city views, a romantic love-lock fence, and a cable car ride.

  • Location: Seoul
  • Best Time: Evening for city lights
  • Activity: Observation deck, stroll in Namsan Park

Jeju Island

A volcanic island famous for its lava tubes, waterfalls, beaches, and the towering Hallasan volcano—Korea's highest peak.

  • Location: Jeju Province
  • Best Time: Spring and Autumn
  • Activity: beach visits, caving

DMZ Tour

Visit the Demilitarized Zone, a sobering reminder of the Korean War; guided tours offer a look at the border and historic sites.

  • Location Northern Gyeonggi Province
  • Requires: Guided tour booking
  • Highlights: Dora Observatory, 3rd Tunnel

Bukchon Hanok Village

Step back in time wandering through streets lined with traditional Korean houses (hanoks) among modern Seoul.

  • Location: Seoul
  • Best Time: Morning for fewer crowds
  • Activity: Photography, cultural centers

Lotte World

One of the world's largest indoor theme parks with rides, parades, and a folk museum showcasing traditional Korean culture.

  • Location: Seoul
  • Best Time: Weekends for events
  • Activity: Fun rides, cultural exhibitions

K-pop Culture

K-pop, short for Korean pop music, is a genre of pop music originating in South Korea and enjoying widespread popularity worldwide. Known for its catchy melodies, impressive choreography, and visually stunning music videos, K-pop has become a cultural phenomenon, influencing fashion, dance, and entertainment around the world, and giving rise to many globally popular idol groups, such as BlackPink, BabyMonster, and BTS.
